Footwear & Accessories

Snowshoes, crampons, pumps, and more

Airlite Inflatable Snowshoes

Our flagship product — the world's first US Patented inflatable emergency snowshoe. Compact, lightweight, and built from extremely tough puncture-resistant material tested in extreme Alaskan winters.

Crampons

Although the traction pad on the snowshoe works well in many snow conditions, we now have crampons available for added traction when needed. These are also included in the Complete Package.

Inflation Devices

Each snowshoe features two valves — a quick inflation/deflation valve and a standard tire valve that most any small hand pump or CO2 device will fit. The preferred method of inflation is to blow up the unit by mouth then give it a quick shot of CO2 or a few strokes of a pump for firmness.

Available Packages

  • Basic Kit: Pair of Airlite snowshoes with drawstring storage bags
  • Complete Package: Snowshoes + crampons + inflation devices + pressure gauge + patch kit
  • Accessories: Replacement crampons, patch kits, pumps, CO2 inflators

Storage Bags

Each pair comes with drawstring bags. You may request a color — we usually have various shades of greens, reds, blues, orange, brown, and occasionally yellow. If you're in no hurry, we can have specific colors available within a few days.
