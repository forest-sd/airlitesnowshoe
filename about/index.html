About Airlite Snowshoes

The world's first inflatable emergency snowshoe

Our Story

Although originally designed for snowmachiners, our marketing research has increased the target market substantially. Light aircraft and bush pilots, dog mushers, mountain rescue groups, State Troopers, Fish and Game officers, National Guard Air Rescue pilots, hunters, skiers — the list of those who have expressed a great deal of interest in this product goes on.

What it boils down to is that anyone who lives, works, or plays where deep snow can become a nuisance, a hazard, or life threatening should have a pair handy.

Why Airlite Matters

Who knows — if those men we've all heard about, who had left their families behind in their stranded snowbound vehicles to try and walk to get help, might still be alive today if they had a pair of these snowshoes tucked away in the trunk.

Our snowshoes are US Patented, made from extremely tough puncture-resistant material, and have been tested in the most extreme Alaskan winters. They're lightweight, compact, and designed to be the emergency tool you hope you never need — but will be grateful you have.

Features

  • US Patented inflatable design
  • Extremely tough, puncture-resistant material
  • Tested in extreme Alaskan conditions
  • Dual valve system — quick inflate/deflate + standard tire valve
  • Compatible with most hand pumps and CO2 devices
  • Includes pressure gauge (inflate to 8.5-10.5 PSI)
  • Traction pad for various snow conditions
  • Optional crampons for added traction
  • Comes with drawstring storage bag
