How It Works

Each snowshoe features two valves — a quick inflation/deflation valve and a standard tire valve compatible with most small hand pumps or CO2 devices.

The preferred method: blow up the unit by mouth, then give it a quick shot of CO2 or a few strokes of a pump for firmness. Inflate until the toe is hard before use — 8.5 to 10.5 PSI, easily checked with the included pressure gauge.

Important: Use of this product without fully inflating is not recommended.
